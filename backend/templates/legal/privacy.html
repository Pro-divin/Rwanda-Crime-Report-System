1. Introduction

The Rwanda Crime Report System (RCRS) is committed to protecting your privacy while maintaining transparency and accountability in crime reporting. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal data.

2. Data Controller Information

  • Regulatory Authority: Rwanda Utilities Regulatory Authority (RURA)
  • Contact: privacy@rcrs.gov.rw

4. How We Use Your Data

4.1 Primary Uses

  1. Law Enforcement Support: Share reports with RNP, RIB, and prosecutor's office
  2. Evidence Preservation: Store media on IPFS; anchor hashes to Cardano blockchain
  3. Report Verification: Generate cryptographic proof of authenticity
  4. System Administration: Maintain infrastructure, prevent abuse, improve performance

4.2 Secondary Uses (Anonymized Data Only)

  • Crime trend analysis and statistics
  • Public safety research and policy development
  • System performance optimization
  • Training machine learning models for fraud detection

6. Data Security Measures

6.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 for data in transit; AES-256 for data at rest
  • Blockchain Immutability: Reports permanently recorded on Cardano
  • IPFS Content Addressing: Cryptographic verification of media files
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions; multi-factor authentication

6.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee training on data protection and confidentiality
  • Incident response plan for data breaches
  • Limited access to personal data (need-to-know basis)

Pursuant to Law N¬∞ 60/2018, you have the right to:

7.1 Access & Correction

  • Access: Request a copy of your submitted reports (email privacy@rcrs.gov.rw)
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data

7.2 Limitations on Deletion

‚ö†Ô∏è BLOCKCHAIN IMMUTABILITY:
Due to the permanent nature of blockchain technology:
  • Report data cannot be deleted once anchored on Cardano
