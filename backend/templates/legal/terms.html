2. System Purpose & Scope

RCRS is a public blockchain-based reporting platform designed to:

  • Enable citizens to report crimes, incidents, and emergencies
  • Provide tamper-proof evidence storage via IPFS and Cardano blockchain
  • Support law enforcement agencies (RNP, RIB) with verified reports
  • Maintain transparency and accountability in Rwanda's justice system

3. Eligibility & User Requirements

3.1 Who Can Use the System

Universal Right to Report: Any person in Rwanda has the right to report crimes, regardless of age, nationality, or relationship to the incident. The system is open to:

  • General Public: Any person aged 18+ or emancipated minors
  • Witnesses: Individuals with first-hand knowledge of incidents
  • Victims: Persons directly affected by crimes
  • Organizations: Legal entities reporting institutional matters
  • Concerned Citizens: Anyone witnessing criminal activity in their community
  • Minors (with Guardian): Youth under 18 may report with parental/guardian consent

  • Corruption, bribery, or embezzlement
  • Fraud, scams, or financial crimes
  • Traffic accidents with injuries or property damage
