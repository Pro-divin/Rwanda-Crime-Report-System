# User Consent Statement
## Rwanda Crime Report System (RCRS)

**Last Updated:** December 13, 2025

---

## Before You Submit a Report

By clicking **"I Agree"** or **"Submit Report"**, you confirm that you have read, understood, and consent to the following:

---

## 1. Purpose of This System

✅ I understand that the Rwanda Crime Report System is designed to:
- Report crimes, emergencies, and suspicious activities
- Provide evidence to Rwanda National Police (RNP) and Rwanda Investigation Bureau (RIB)
- Create permanent, immutable records using blockchain technology

---

## 2. Data Collection and Usage

✅ I consent to the collection and use of:
- My report details (crime type, description, location)
- Media evidence (photos, videos)
- GPS coordinates (if I enable location sharing)
- Device information (IP address, browser type)
- Contact information (if I provide it)

✅ I understand this data will be used to:
- Investigate crimes
- Coordinate emergency response
- Provide evidence in legal proceedings
- Generate anonymous statistics

---

## 3. Data Sharing with Authorities

✅ I consent to sharing my report with:
- **Rwanda National Police (RNP)**
- **Rwanda Investigation Bureau (RIB)**
- **Courts and judicial authorities**
- **Other authorized government agencies**

✅ I understand that:
- Law enforcement will have full access to my report
- Authorities may contact me if I provided contact information
- My report may be used as evidence in criminal proceedings

---

## 4. Blockchain and IPFS Technology

✅ I understand and accept that:
- My evidence will be stored on **IPFS** (InterPlanetary File System), a distributed network
- Evidence hashes will be anchored to **Cardano blockchain**
- **These records are PERMANENT and CANNOT be deleted**
- IPFS content may be accessible via public gateways
- Blockchain transactions are publicly verifiable (but do not contain personal information)

✅ I acknowledge that:
- Once submitted, I **cannot edit or delete** blockchain/IPFS records
- Evidence will exist permanently for legal and investigative purposes
- This permanence is intentional to ensure evidence integrity

---

## 5. Media Capture and Use Policy

✅ I confirm that the photos and videos I submit are:
- **Truthful and accurate** representations of the incident
- **Not altered, edited, or manipulated** to deceive
- **Relevant** to the crime or emergency I am reporting
- **Taken lawfully** without violating others' rights

✅ I understand that:
- Media is being submitted **solely for reporting crimes/emergencies**
- Illegal use (harassment, defamation, privacy violation) is **prohibited**
- Misuse may result in **legal action under Rwandan law**

✅ I confirm I am **NOT** using this system to:
- ❌ Harm, threaten, or harass individuals
- ❌ Violate privacy rights
- ❌ Create false evidence
- ❌ Defame or damage reputations
- ❌ Exploit vulnerable persons

---

## 6. Accuracy and Truthfulness

✅ I swear/affirm that:
- The information I am providing is **true and accurate** to the best of my knowledge
- I am **not submitting a false report**
- I understand that **false reporting is a criminal offense** in Rwanda

✅ I acknowledge that:
- Deliberately misleading authorities may result in **prosecution**
- False reports waste valuable law enforcement resources
- I may be held **criminally and civilly liable** for false information

---

## 7. Anonymous Reporting

If I choose to report anonymously:

✅ I understand that:
- My name, email, and phone number will **not** be collected
- I will receive a **reference code** to track my report
- I **cannot edit or delete** the report after submission
- Law enforcement may still collect technical data (IP address, device info)

✅ I accept that:
- Anonymous reports may have limitations in follow-up communication
- Authorities may use technical means to verify authenticity if necessary

---

## 8. Privacy and Data Protection

✅ I have read and understood the **Privacy Policy**

✅ I consent to:
- Collection of my personal data (if provided)
- Storage in databases hosted in Rwanda
- Use of cookies for session management
- Generation of anonymous statistics

✅ I understand my rights under **Law N° 60/2018** (Rwanda Data Protection Law):
- Right to access my data
- Right to correct inaccurate data
- Right to file complaints with RURA

---

## 9. Evidence Integrity and Court Use

✅ I understand that:
- My report may be used as **evidence in court**
- Blockchain anchoring provides **cryptographic proof** of authenticity
- Judges and lawyers may reference my evidence
- I may be called as a **witness** if I provided contact information

✅ I acknowledge that:
- Evidence cannot be tampered with once anchored
- This protects both victims and accused from evidence manipulation

---

## 10. System Availability and Limitations

✅ I understand that:
- The system is provided "as is"
- Technical issues may temporarily prevent submissions
- Submitting a report does **not guarantee police action**
- Response times depend on crime severity and resources

✅ I acknowledge that:
- I should call **112** for immediate life-threatening emergencies
- This system is supplementary to traditional reporting methods

---

## 11. Terms and Conditions

✅ I have read and agree to the **Terms and Conditions**

✅ I understand that:
- These terms are governed by **Rwandan law**
- Disputes will be resolved in Rwandan courts
- The system may be updated, and I will be notified of changes

---

## 12. Legal Disclaimer

✅ I acknowledge that:
- The Government of Rwanda is **not liable** for:
  - Delays in police response
  - Actions or inactions of law enforcement
  - Consequences of my false reporting
- I am **solely responsible** for the content I submit

---

## 13. Consequences of Misuse

✅ I understand that **misuse of this system** may result in:
- ⚖️ Criminal prosecution under Rwandan law
- ⚖️ Civil lawsuits for defamation or privacy violations
- ⚖️ Suspension or ban from the system
- ⚖️ Cooperation with authorities to identify me

---

## 14. Voluntary Submission

✅ I confirm that:
- I am submitting this report **voluntarily**
- I have not been coerced or threatened
- I am of sound mind and legal age (18+) or reporting in an emergency

---

## 15. Contact and Support

✅ I understand that I can contact:
- **Privacy Officer:** privacy@rcrs.gov.rw
- **Support Team:** support@rcrs.gov.rw
- **Emergency Hotline:** 112
