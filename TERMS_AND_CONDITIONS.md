## 2. System Purpose

The Rwanda Crime Report System is an official platform designed to:
- Enable citizens to report crimes, emergencies, and suspicious activities
- Facilitate rapid response by Rwanda National Police (RNP) and Rwanda Investigation Bureau (RIB)
- Preserve evidence using blockchain and distributed storage technology
- Maintain transparent, immutable records for justice and accountability

### 6.1 Immutability
- Reports anchored to **Cardano blockchain** are **permanent and immutable**
- Evidence stored on **IPFS** is distributed globally and **cannot be deleted**
- You acknowledge that submitted content becomes part of a permanent record

### 6.2 Public Accessibility
- IPFS content may be accessible via public gateways
- Blockchain transactions are publicly verifiable
- Your identity remains protected unless you provide contact information

### 6.3 No Deletion Guarantee
